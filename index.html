Few Words About Us

SDMS is a Mechanical Engineering based startup that focuses on real life problems of technical students and solving them through practical knowledge.

WE, TEAM SDMS are alumnus of SRKR ENGINEERING COLLEGE , Bhimavaram. Coming from a humble background, we always saw the parallels between automation in Village area and cities- The whirring of the wheel caps on cemented roads and the vigorous rotation of a potter's wheel on a sanded surface looked similar to us. we could see the divide created by the society being bridged by physics. This made us realize the power and beauty of technology .we started to realize that cars weren't exactly perfect. They caused pollution and harming the environment. This disturbed us deeply and prompted us to work out a method to rectify this flaw in automobiles through electrifying the motors which cause pollutants on combustion. The collective efforts of passionate engineers, with their vigorous pursuit of solving the real life problems is responsible for this company formation

Key areas we are currently focusing are

  • University level club formation.
  • Motorsport challenges.
  • Technical support through unique workshops.
  • Motorsports vehicle parts supply.
